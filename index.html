Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship outside Lagos?

Yes, we offer nationwide shipping across Nigeria and select international destinations. Shipping fees apply.

Can I customize the style?

For certain designs, customization options are available. Please contact us on WhatsApp to discuss your specific requirements.

What is your return policy?

Due to the bespoke nature of our products, all sales are final. However, we ensure quality and fit before dispatch.

How long does an order take?

Production and delivery times vary depending on the complexity of the design and your location. Typically, orders are delivered within 2-4 weeks.
